Everyday Fitness Lifters Meet

The semi annual lifters meet held by our gym, Everyday Fitness is set to take place in September 1st. The tournaments will take place here at Everyday Fitness Gym. The Meet will consist of three main Lifting tournaments Squats, Deadlifts and Bench Press. All Three lifts are divided to four categories below 60 kgs, 60-70kgs , 70-80kgs and above 80kgs. There will also be different categories for male and female lifters.
